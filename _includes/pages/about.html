About Us

A revolution in drug discovery & development

Drug development is time consuming, resource intensive and fails 90% of the time. A more predictive lead optimization process can lower the failure rate by improving effectiveness, lowering toxicity and increasing the absorption of drugs.

Our Mission

HTuO Biosciences is a deep tech company, working at the interface of chemistry and computer programming, to develop an advanced molecular simulation capable of accurately predicting the behaviour of molecules for the purpose of drug development.

Identifying Drug Candidates

Drug development is costly and risky, with a 90% failure rate. HTuO's physics-based molecular modeling solution will reduce time and resources needed to develop drugs.

It all starts with Physics

By starting with the physics of individual atoms, we have developed a brand new energy equation for molecular systems, which allows us to derive a new and accurate force field.

Differentiation

HTuO is an innovative tool that can accurately simulate and calculate properties, making it an invaluable resource for drug designers.
