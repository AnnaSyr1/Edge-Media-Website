Detailed Schedule - Project Milestones and Testing Strategy

By the end of September

Finish app functionality (Saoud, Jessika)
To test the app's functionality, we will connect the app to the Arduino via BLE and test all our app features to make sure that everything works as expected.

Finish smart clothing first working prototype (Rolando)
Our first working prototype will not have all the features; it will probably only include the LED leaf. To test it, we will use our app to change the LED color (make sure nothing explodes or burns).

Finish smart shoes (Hyrum)
To test our smart shoes, we will ensure that it automatically self-ties when it detects someone wearing the shoes.

By the end of October

Finish the base build for our smart wardrobe (Rolando, Hyrum, Saoud - if needed)
To test the wardrobe, we will make sure it's solid enough for moving and safe to use.

Get feedback on our first smart clothing design and refine accordingly (everyone)
We will test our refined design similarly to our first prototype, adding all other leaves, and ensuring the app can control all leaves.

By the end of November

Finish smart wardrobe with all features (everyone)
We will test our wardrobe by ensuring it's safe to use, can charge, and store leaves easily.

Finish smart clothing final design (everyone)
We will test our final design to ensure all parts' placements are final and all leaves are easily swappable.

December

Prepare for the final demo (everyone)
