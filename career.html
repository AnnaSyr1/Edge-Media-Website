HTuO Biosciences is located in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada, a city recognized for its beauty, livability and diversity. Boasting Canada's best climate, with mild winters and temperate summers, as well as close proximity to the skiing in the Coast mountains and the beaches on the Pacific ocean, Vancouver is a unique city balanced between urban life and nature.

We recognize that the best talent is scattered around the world and, due to covid, not everyone is able to relocate to Vancouver right now. If you're interested in working for HTuO Biosciences remotely, please reach out to let us know you're interested in our positions, and include a note on your ability or schedule for relocating. We will consider fully remote positions for the right candidate.

All positions listed will remain open until filled.
